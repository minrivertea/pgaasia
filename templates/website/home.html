Welcome to the Asia-China PGA Discussion forum!

Participate in discussions and decisions that will shape the final frontier of the golfing industry - China!

Our mandate is to allow open and fair discussion of important issues and consider the views of PGA professionals and intelligent members of the golf community at large.

Important Note: any references to subjects deemed politically sensitive by the Chinese government will be removed. We ask users to use their discretion when discussing culturally sensitive issues, and in the long-term, this will ensure our website remains focussed on golf, and visible and accessible for local Chinese and international users. Please treat this unique arena wisely!
